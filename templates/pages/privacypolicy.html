Privacy Policy

Your data privacy and security are critical to building your empire.

Last Updated: April 2026

At Empire Builder GPT, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website, application, and AI-powered services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience and most accurate startup architectures, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, username, phone number, and location details.
  • User-Generated Content: The startup ideas, business prompts, and descriptions you submit to our AI engine.
  • Profile Data: Avatars, profile images, and other optional biography details you provide.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including features used, time spent, and diagnostic data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect primarily to provide, maintain, and improve Empire Builder GPT. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Process your prompts through our AI models to generate tailored business plans and tech stacks.
  • Save and organize your startup ideas securely in your personal dashboard.
  • Authenticate your identity and protect against unauthorized access.
  • Send you important updates regarding your account or changes to our platform.

3. AI Processing & Idea Protection

We understand that your startup ideas are highly valuable. The prompts you enter are processed securely to generate your blueprints. We do not claim ownership of your generated startup ideas. While your inputs are sent to our language model providers to generate the responses, they are transmitted via secure APIs and are not used by third parties to train public models without consent.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, AI API providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, subpoena, or other legal processes to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure socket layer (SSL) technology, to protect your personal data and saved ideas from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can manage your profile details directly from your dashboard. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated startup ideas, please contact our support team.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
